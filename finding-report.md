## Target Audited
Smartphone

## Summary
I carried out a basic cybersecurity audit on my smartphone to identify common security weaknesses.

## Findings
1. The phone has a PIN and fingerprint lock enabled.
2. The device is not fully updated.
3. App installations from unknown sources are disabled.
4. Some apps have excessive permissions.
5. Two-factor authentication is not enabled on all important apps.
6. Public Wi-Fi is occasionally used without protection.

## Risk Level
Medium Risk

## Why?
Although basic security measures are in place, outdated software and weak account protection increase the risk of cyber attacks.
